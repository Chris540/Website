Mobile Hospital Waiting Time App

Objective: Design an interface to improve a patient's visit to the hospital.

Constraints: Variety of users:

  • Low ability, high motivation
  • Low ability, low motivation
  • High ability, low motivation
  • High ability, high motivation

Requirements:

  • Motivate users to use app consistently
  • Simple Interface for ease of use
  • Allow
  • High ability, high motivation
Show more >>

The app needed to be simple to use, aid the user at a critical time of need, and also minimize the number of “hospital hoppers” (people who go to hospitals without sufficient reason). It also needed to address some of the problems hospital staff observed of visitors such as parking and location of facilities.

A really important factor into the design was to not discourage people from coming to the hospital, but at the same time, offer the user a shorter wait time. This meant that our design had to incorporate some techniques which may seem more troublesome to the more technologically inclined user, but was vital in order to encourage users to choose the right hospital.
